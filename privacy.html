Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Palestine Support Hub is designed to be a privacy-focused tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your Browse activity.

What Data is Stored and Why?

The extension stores a small amount of data on your computer for functionality. This data is never sent to us or any third party.

  • Theme Preference: To remember your chosen theme (Light, Dark, or Green), we use chrome.storage.sync. This is synced with your Google account so your theme preference is consistent across your devices, but it is not accessible to us.
  • News & Content Cache: To make the extension load faster, we temporarily store news articles, the current "Word of the Hour," and the current Quran verse using chrome.storage.local. This data is stored only on your local machine.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any analytics, track your Browse history, or log your interactions with the extension.

Third-Party Services

When you click on a link within the extension (such as a news article or a link to an activist group), you will be directed to an external website. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to read them.

The extension fetches news from public RSS feeds. This process involves your browser making a direct request to those news services, which is standard internet behavior.
