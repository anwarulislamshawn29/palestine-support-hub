Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is my data and Browse activity safe?

Yes. The extension is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect or track any of your personal data or Browse history. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.

How is the news sourced?

The news is aggregated from publicly available RSS feeds from trusted sources like Al Jazeera, Palestine Chronicle, Mondoweiss, and The Electronic Intifada. The extension fetches this information directly from these sources when you open it.

How do the "Word of the Hour" and "Quran Verse" features work?

The extension contains a predefined list of words and verses. A background timer automatically cycles to the next item in the list every hour and saves it locally on your computer for the popup to display.
