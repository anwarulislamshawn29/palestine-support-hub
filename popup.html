What Can You Do?

  • Educate & Amplify: Share information from reliable sources and amplify the voices of Palestinian journalists, artists, and activists.
  • Contact Representatives: Call and email your elected officials to demand they support Palestinian human rights and international law.
  • Support Ethical Commerce: Participate in the BDS movement and make a conscious effort to support Palestinian-owned businesses.
  • Join Local Actions: Find and join peaceful protests, vigils, and community events to show your solidarity.
  • Engage with Culture: Read books, watch films, and listen to music by Palestinian creators to understand and celebrate their stories.
  • Challenge Misinformation: Respectfully correct falsehoods and provide context in conversations with friends, family, and online.
